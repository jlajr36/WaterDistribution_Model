In **SysML (and UML) block/class diagrams**, the **diamond symbols** indicate **whole–part relationships** between elements. The **color of the diamond** tells you how strong the ownership is.

## 1. Black Diamond ◆ — Composition (Strong Ownership)

* **Meaning:** Strong **whole–part** relationship.
* The **whole owns the parts**.
* If the **whole is deleted, the parts are deleted too**.
* A part usually **cannot exist independently** of the whole. ([<b>SysML Forum</b>][1])

**Example**

```
Car ◆── Engine
```

Interpretation:

* The **Engine is a part of the Car**
* If the **Car is removed, the Engine instance goes away with it**

**Key idea:** *Lifecycle dependency*

Common SysML usage:

* System → subsystem
* Aircraft → wing
* Computer → CPU

---

## 2. White Diamond ◇ — Shared Aggregation (Weak Ownership)

* **Meaning:** Weak **whole–part** relationship.
* The part **can exist independently** of the whole.
* The part **can potentially be shared by multiple wholes**. ([<b>SysML Forum</b>][1])

**Example**

```
Team ◇── Player
```

Interpretation:

* A **Team has Players**
* But a **Player can exist without that team** (or move to another team)

**Key idea:** *Reference or shared component*

---

## Quick Comparison

| Symbol          | Name            | Ownership | Lifecycle                    | Example       |
| --------------- | --------------- | --------- | ---------------------------- | ------------- |
| ◆ Black Diamond | **Composition** | Strong    | Part dies with whole         | Car → Engine  |
| ◇ White Diamond | **Aggregation** | Weak      | Part can exist independently | Team → Player |

---

✅ **Rule of thumb used in SysML modeling:**

* **Black diamond = “part of” the system structure**
* **White diamond = “has a / shared with” relationship**

---

💡 **Tip for SysML Block Definition Diagrams (BDD):**
Most **physical system hierarchies use black diamonds** because subsystems are usually **true parts** of the system.

Example:

```
Aircraft ◆── Wing
Aircraft ◆── Engine
Aircraft ◆── Avionics
```
