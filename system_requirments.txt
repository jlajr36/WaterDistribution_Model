### **UR_10 → SR_10: Bacteria Removal System Requirement**

* **System Requirement:**
  The water treatment system shall include one or more **bacteria removal mechanisms** (e.g., ultrafiltration, UV disinfection, chemical treatment) capable of achieving a **minimum 5-log (≥99.999%) reduction** of viable bacteria under nominal operating conditions.
* **Verification Method:**

  * Laboratory challenge tests using representative bacterial strains.
  * Measure input and output concentrations and confirm ≥5-log reduction.

---

### **UR_11 → SR_11: Parasite Removal System Requirement**

* **System Requirement:**
  The water treatment system shall include **mechanisms effective against protozoa and helminths**, capable of achieving a **minimum 3-log (≥99.9%) reduction** under nominal operating conditions.
* **Verification Method:**

  * Use surrogate parasites or certified test organisms in controlled tests.
  * Measure reduction in output water to confirm ≥3-log reduction.

---

### **UR_12 → SR_12: Contaminant Disposal System Requirement**

* **System Requirement:**
  The water treatment system shall incorporate a **contaminant handling module** that collects, isolates, and disposes of removed bacteria, parasites, and other contaminants **in compliance with all local and international environmental regulations**.
* **Verification Method:**

  * Audit the disposal process for regulatory compliance.
  * Inspect containment integrity and confirm proper treatment/disposal of waste.
