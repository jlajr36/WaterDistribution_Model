*   **Start and Initialization:** The flow begins at a solid black **initial node** and moves to an action labeled **`tankDrainTime_test`**.

*   **Decision Logic:** Based on the water level reading, the system evaluates three specific conditions:

    *   **Low Level:** If the level is **less than or equal to "low"**, the system triggers the action **`: Turn Pump On`**.
