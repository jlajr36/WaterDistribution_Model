This is an **Activity Diagram** outlining the procedural steps for **Safe Contaminant Disposal**. The process is designed to ensure that all waste materials are handled according to regulatory standards before final disposal.

The workflow consists of the following detailed steps:

*   **Initial Collection:** The process begins with the **collection of contaminants** directly from treatment units.
*   **Containment:** Once collected, these contaminants are **isolated in a safe container** to prevent leakage or exposure.
*   **Regulatory Validation:** A critical step follows where the system must **check compliance with environmental regulations**.
